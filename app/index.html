PastPort

Your window to the past

Why use PastPort?

An interactive and immersive experience which takes you into the past so you can glimpse at what life was like.

How does PastPort work?

PastPort will show you content based on your where you are and what you're looking at.

Where can I use it?

Designated points around the city will have historical content you can experience. The app will notice you are in a location of with photos, video or audio available and notify you.

Who is it for?

PastPort is for everyone, whether you have an interest in history or just want to see what Wellington looked like last centuary; PastPort offers a unique view on the world around you.
